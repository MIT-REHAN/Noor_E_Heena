The Artist Behind the Henna

Meet Noor

At Noor_e_Heena, every design tells a story. With a passion for intricate artistry and years of experience, Noor creates personalized mehendi designs that celebrate love, tradition, and life's most beautiful moments.

From bridal masterpieces to festive celebrations, each design is crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence. We use only premium organic, chemical-free henna to ensure a rich, dark, and long-lasting color.
